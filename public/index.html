Gif It To Me

Rules of the Game:

One player will create a game room with a selected number of players and a game room name of their choice with the ‘Make a Game’ inputs and then by clicking the ‘Start Game’ button. Once that room has been created, all other players are allowed to join by entering the name of said room into the ‘Join a Game’ input and clicking the ‘Join Game’ button.

The first round begins once all players have clicked the ‘Ready’ button. The first judge will be randomly selected. A gif will be displayed for all of the non-judges to caption, and a timer will count down the amount of time remaining for them to think of something to write. Once the timer runs out, all captions are automatically submitted to the judge. The judge then selects their favorite, and whoever wins gets to be the judge for the next round.

There are 10 spaces. The first player to make it to the 10th space is the game winner.
